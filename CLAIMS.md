# Defensible Claims

## Claim 1
Scalar topological summaries such as `topo_max` are insufficient for structurally non-trivial loops.

## Claim 2
H1-profile representation improves multi-loop and figure-eight detection by preserving multi-bar structure.

## Claim 3
Temporal features reduce false positives on transient and shrinking-loop regimes.

## Claim 4
Online triggering should be classifier-gated, not purely rule-based.

## Negative result
Hard near-miss / spiral rejection is dangerous as a pre-alert rule because it can suppress true figure-eight and multi-loop trajectories.
